Our Story: 15 Years of Honest Medicare Guidance

Medicare can be confusing, overwhelming, and honestly, a bit scary. Most people don't understand the options. Too many feel pressured by insurance agents focused on commissions rather than their actual needs. Worse, they're dealing with aging parents, their own health concerns, and the stress of making the wrong choice that could cost thousands.

In 2011, Susana Marcos started Medicare California with a simple mission: help people navigate Medicare with clarity, honesty, and genuine care. Not because she had to. Because she believed everyone deserves guidance they can trust.

Susana Marcos

Founder | Medicare Expert | Licensed Agent

Susana has spent 15 years helping Medicare beneficiaries find the right plans. She started in a small town of 5,000 in Missouri, working one-on-one with seniors to understand their needs, their fears, and their specific situations. Her reputation grew entirely through word-of-mouth referrals. Today, she's helped over 1,200 clients navigate Medicare and has built trust that spans eight states: California, Missouri, Kansas, Oklahoma, Iowa, Illinois, Texas, and Florida.

Susana is bilingual in English and Spanish, bringing deep cultural understanding to her work. Her philosophy is simple: "Cada cliente es familia"—each client is family. She never pressures. She educates. She listens. She recommends only what makes sense for that individual's situation.

Why Trust Matters in Medicare

Medicare decisions aren't like shopping for other products. People often feel scared and confused because:

It's Complex

Four parts, dozens of plans, changing rules every year. Even smart people get overwhelmed trying to understand the options.

The Stakes Are High

The wrong choice can cost thousands in out-of-pocket expenses. People rightly worry about making a mistake that affects their health and finances.

Aging Parents Create Stress

Adult children often help parents navigate Medicare while managing their own lives. It's emotional and time-consuming.

Too Many Sales Pitches

Insurance agents make commissions on sales. How do you know who's prioritizing your needs versus their paycheck?

That's why Medicare California exists. You deserve an advisor who listens to your situation, understands your doctors and medications, asks about your budget, and recommends the best plan for you specifically. Not the plan with the highest commission. The plan that makes sense for your life.
