Susana Marcos Insurance LLC ("we," "us") operates medicare-california.com. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you interact with our site or with one of our licensed agents.

Who we are

Medicare California is operated by Susana Marcos Insurance LLC, a licensed insurance agency in California. We help Medicare-eligible individuals compare and enroll in Medicare Advantage, Medigap, and Part D plans at no cost. We have served California seniors for over 15 years.

Information we collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide through our website forms or during consultations with our agents. This may include:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • ZIP code and date of birth
  • Health coverage preferences and current insurance status
  • State of residence

We also collect standard web analytics data (pages visited, time on site, device type) through privacy-respecting analytics tools. We do not use invasive tracking or third-party advertising cookies.

How we use your information

Your information is used to:

  • Match you with Medicare plans that fit your needs and location
  • Contact you about plan options you have requested
  • Schedule consultations with our licensed agents
  • Improve our website and services

We do not use your data for purposes unrelated to Medicare enrollment assistance.

Information sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared only with licensed insurance carriers for the purpose of plan enrollment, and only when you have given consent to proceed with an application.

Data security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure data storage. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us. California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for site functionality, such as remembering your language preference. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

Third-party links

Our site may contain links to external websites (such as Medicare.gov or insurance carrier sites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

Children's privacy

Our services are intended for adults aged 65 and older (or those approaching Medicare eligibility). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
